Patrick Pearse (Irish: Pádraig Anraí Mac Piarais)

Pádraig Mac Piarais sits for his portrait.

Pádraig Mac Piarais was one of the leaders of the 1916 Easter Rising in Dublin, Ireland. For his part in the conflict, he was executed on May 3, 1916.
